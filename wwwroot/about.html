About the KWRIS 2.0 Map Explorer

The KWRIS 2.0 Map Explorer is a website for map-based access to spatial data from the Advanced Centre for Integrated Water Resources Management (ACIWRM), under the Water Resources Depatment of Government of Karnataka.

The State is developing the Karnataka Water Resource Information and Knowledge System (KWRIS) with a draft strategy and plan for its on-going development and implementation.

KWRIS hydro-met, groundwater and water quality data content, monitoring and data capture networks are being improved with assistance from the ADB, World Bank and other partners.

The KWRIS will be used to support most of the ACIWRM activities described below. In particular, it will lead to a spatially based Inventory of River Basins in Karnataka; River basin and sub-basin plans; water policy studies; monitoring and reportingof water resource condition; and, research and investigations.

The KWRIS will comprise a spatial system bringing together quantity-quality-condition aspects of surface water, groundwater and water dependent ecosystems. A decision support system with GIS and integrated river basin modelling approaches is in preparationand it will be used for IWRM activities is to form part of the system. An expertmodelling group is being established to support activities.

To see what data is available on the Map Explorer, refer to the Data Catalogue in the Map Explorer itself. Click the Data Catalogue tab and expand the Data Set group names.

How to use the Map Explorer

Just go to https://kwris.aciwrm.org/. Most operations are obvious, but you can click the Help and FAQs button near the top of this page for specific instructions.

For further information email kwris@aciwrm.org. Feel free to send us feedback and bug reports.
